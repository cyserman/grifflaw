## Color Combinations (for reference)

### Header/Navigation
- **Background:** Navy Blue (#1e3a5f)
- **Text:** Off-White (#f7fafc)
- **Hover:** Dark Blue (#2c5282)

### Body Content
- **Background:** Off-White (#f7fafc)
- **Text:** Charcoal Gray (#2d3748)
- **Links:** Steel Blue (#4299e1)

### Callout Boxes
- **Background:** Light Gray (#e2e8f0)
- **Border:** Off-Blue (#4a7ba7)
- **Text:** Charcoal Gray (#2d3748)

### Footer
- **Background:** Dark Blue (#2c5282)
- **Text:** Off-White (#f7fafc)
- **Links:** Off-White with hover underline

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Navy Blue background, white text
- **Secondary:** Off-Blue background, white text
- **Hover:** Dark Blue background (both)

---

**Note:** All color combinations are tested for WCAG AA accessibility compliance.
