# Griffiths Law - Style Guide

## Color Palette

Professional gray and blue color scheme for sophisticated legal branding.

### Primary Colors

**Navy Blue** (Primary Brand Color)
- Hex: `#1e3a5f`
- RGB: `rgb(30, 58, 95)`
- Use: Headers, navigation, primary buttons, links

**Dark Blue** (Secondary)
- Hex: `#2c5282`
- RGB: `rgb(44, 82, 130)`
- Use: Subheadings, accents, hover states

**Off-Blue** (Accent)
- Hex: `#4a7ba7`
- RGB: `rgb(74, 123, 167)`
- Use: Highlights, callout boxes, secondary buttons

### Neutral Grays

**Charcoal Gray** (Dark Text)
- Hex: `#2d3748`
- RGB: `rgb(45, 55, 72)`
- Use: Body text, main content

**Medium Gray** (Secondary Text)
- Hex: `#718096`
- RGB: `rgb(113, 128, 150)`
- Use: Captions, metadata, secondary information

**Light Gray** (Backgrounds)
- Hex: `#e2e8f0`
- RGB: `rgb(226, 232, 240)`
- Use: Section backgrounds, cards, dividers

**Off-White** (Primary Background)
- Hex: `#f7fafc`
- RGB: `rgb(247, 250, 252)`
- Use: Main background, content areas

### Accent Colors

**Steel Blue** (Info/Links)
- Hex: `#4299e1`
- RGB: `rgb(66, 153, 225)`
- Use: Hyperlinks, informational callouts

**Slate Gray** (Borders)
- Hex: `#cbd5e0`
- RGB: `rgb(203, 213, 224)`
- Use: Borders, dividing lines, subtle separators

---

## Typography

### Headings
- **Font:** Georgia, serif (or similar professional serif)
- **Color:** Navy Blue (#1e3a5f)
- **Weight:** 600-700 (Semi-bold to Bold)

### Body Text
- **Font:** -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, sans-serif
- **Color:** Charcoal Gray (#2d3748)
- **Weight:** 400 (Regular)
- **Line Height:** 1.7

### Links
- **Color:** Steel Blue (#4299e1)
- **Hover:** Dark Blue (#2c5282)
- **Underline:** On hover only

---

## Usage Guidelines

### Background Colors by Section

**Header/Navigation**
- Background: Navy Blue (#1e3a5f)
- Text: Off-White (#f7fafc)

**Main Content Area**
- Background: Off-White (#f7fafc)
- Text: Charcoal Gray (#2d3748)

**Sidebar/Featured Content**
- Background: Light Gray (#e2e8f0)
- Text: Charcoal Gray (#2d3748)

**Footer**
- Background: Dark Blue (#2c5282)
- Text: Off-White (#f7fafc)

**Blog Post Cards**
- Background: White (#ffffff)
- Border: Slate Gray (#cbd5e0)
- Hover: Light Gray background (#e2e8f0)

**Callout Boxes/Quotes**
- Background: Light Gray (#e2e8f0)
- Border-Left: Off-Blue (#4a7ba7) - 4px solid
- Text: Charcoal Gray (#2d3748)

**Buttons (Primary)**
- Background: Navy Blue (#1e3a5f)
- Text: White
- Hover: Dark Blue (#2c5282)

**Buttons (Secondary)**
- Background: Off-Blue (#4a7ba7)
- Text: White
- Hover: Dark Blue (#2c5282)

---

## Visual Hierarchy

1. **Headlines:** Navy Blue, bold, large
2. **Subheadings:** Dark Blue, semi-bold, medium
3. **Body Text:** Charcoal Gray, regular
4. **Captions/Meta:** Medium Gray, smaller
5. **Links:** Steel Blue, underline on hover

---

## Accessibility

All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards for contrast:
- Navy Blue text on Off-White background: ✓
- Charcoal Gray text on Off-White background: ✓
- White text on Navy Blue background: ✓
- White text on Dark Blue background: ✓
